ABOUT REHA

REAL ESTATE Housing Authority Lahore is “Nationally recognized corporate” organization that has endeavored to provide,to the people of Pakistan, an opportunity to live the innovative models of modern living.Tuned to the latest worldwide developments in the field of Urban and Community Management, it has added the grandeur of our cities and communities.Relying on the elements of strategic urban planning, development and sustainability it has carvedout its residential and commercial projects.By introducing modern designs in construction of houses, infrastructure and essential associated facilities it has infused a new life in “Luxurious Living”, that is beautifully energetic, attractively vibrant and conveniently livable.Our emphasis continues to remain on building our communities with the essence of our socio-cultural and religious traditions & needs.We envision our future urban dwellings to be more friendly, modern and green.
