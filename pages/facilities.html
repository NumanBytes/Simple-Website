Schools

REHA Education System is a group of progressive and ever-expanding Schools and Colleges quality education to the wards of residents of REHA and surrounding areas. Since its inception in 1993,REHA Education System, endowed with well laid out campuses, a wide range of co/extra curricular activities and state of the art facilities, has endeavored to implement best and innovative educational practices thereby heading towards excellence. REHA Education System is unique in the sense that it offers learning progression to all grade levels.Here we value everyone’s contribution towards the students success and acknowledge and celebrate the diversity, dignity and success of all individuals. The system’s core values are Courtesy, Responsibility and Tolerance. These demonstrate our commitment to creating a culture of learning, co-operation and collaboration.REHA aims at providing quality education to our children in the primary and secondary education. For the completion of this aim, we consult best schools in our society to provide quality education.We have more three best schools in our society which are grooming our children with standard level of teaching.
